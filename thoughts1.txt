functional requirments:-
    -User Management: Add, update, delete students and courses; Assign students to courses
    -Performance Tracking: Record marks/grades; View individual and course performance
    -Data Analysis & Reports: Generate student-wise and course-wise reports; Basic visualization
    with matplotlib
    -CLI Interface: Menu-driven CLI with commands for add/search/update/report generation


students:-
    -can apply for 6 courses
    -view the courses they are enrolled in and the status of their courses, i.e if the course they have applied 
        for has been alloted to them by the admin. the stauts includes:-rejected, accepted and pending status
    -after enrolling(i.e applying and then getting accepted). the students can start tracking their 
        performances for the courses they have enrolled in.
    -can apply for courses during certain period
    
admin:-
    -approve the applications of students into courses
    -add courses for the departments
    -assign teachers to the courses

teachers:-
    -view students records for their course
    -see the trends of the students for their course, using graphs
    -download the records in pdf and exel formats or text file

system:-
    -automatically generate unique id's for the users(both teachers and students) during the signup
